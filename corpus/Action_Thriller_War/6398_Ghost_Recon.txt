After successfully ending an ultranationalist occupation in Norway, Army Rangers Dalton Hibbard and Joe Booth are recruited to Ghost squad. 3 months later, Hibbard and Booth are deployed to Moscow, Russia to deal with the leader of the ultranationalists, General Maxim Cherskiy. After being ambushed and separated from their group, the 2 Ghosts must fight their way through the city if they're to stop Cherskiy and save Russia.
