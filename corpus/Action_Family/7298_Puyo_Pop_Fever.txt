Somewhere, in a far off world called Primp Town, there lives a young girl whose wish is to become a great magic user. Her name is Amitie, and she goes to Primp Magic School to learn about magic spells. However, one day, her teacher, Professor Accord, loses the flying can that she always carries around with her. She says that whoever finds the can gets a reward. Excited, everyone goes out to search, Amitie included. You are Amitie, and you must help her best her classmates, plus a few other faces, at games of Puyo Pop.
