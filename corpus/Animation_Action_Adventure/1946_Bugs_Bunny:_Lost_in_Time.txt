Bugs Bunny takes that wrong turn at Albuquerque and winds up in a Time Machine that takes him to Nowhere. There he meets Merlin Monroe, a local wizard, who sends Bugs on a mission throughout time to locate clocks for his Time Machine. On the way Bugs meets up with Marvin the Martian, Witch Hazel, Rocky & Mugsy, Yosemite Sam and Elmer Fudd in levels taken straight from their classic cartoons.
