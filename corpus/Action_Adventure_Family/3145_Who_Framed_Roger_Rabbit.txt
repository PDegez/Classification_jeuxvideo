Cartoon star Roger Rabbit has been framed for the murder of Marvin Acme, so with the help of detective Eddie Valiant, they set out to prove Roger's innocence, however the evil Judge Doom and his gang of nasty weasels are out to stop them. With the help of Jessica and Benny the Cab, Eddie and Roger whiz through the streets of L.A. and Toon Town and finally, a showdown with Doom.
