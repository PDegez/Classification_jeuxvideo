It's war! The evil mad scientist Andross has unleashed a huge army against his former home world in the bid for galactic conquest. General Pepper has summoned the Star Fox team as backup support to saving Corneria and the Lylat System. Now in 3D, you'll pilot your Arwing through space and skies, drive your Landmaster over terrains and helm your Blue-Marine through the sea, reliving the classic sci-fi shooter once again.
