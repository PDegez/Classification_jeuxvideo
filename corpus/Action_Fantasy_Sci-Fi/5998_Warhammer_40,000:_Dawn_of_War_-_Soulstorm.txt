This game's plot centres in an imperium of man star system called Kaurava, the system is destabilised by a Warp Maelstrom which plunges the system further into Chaos, Many 'Warhammer 40,000' Races join the fray whether they were already in the system like the Orks, Imperial Guard and Necrons, or traveled to it through various means like Eldar Webway or Space Marine Warp Travel. This particular expansion introduces the Imperium of Man's Pious, Fanatical Sisters of Battle and the fallen, Murderous Hedonites of the Dark Eldar.
