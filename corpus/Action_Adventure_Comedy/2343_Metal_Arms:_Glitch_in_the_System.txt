Metal Arms: Glitch in the System is set on the planet Ironstar, a world solely inhabited by a friendly race of robots known as Droids. In an attempt to upgrade himself, the Droid's scientist known as Exavolt accidentally creates General Corrosive, a war mongering robot bent on the enslavement of the Droids. Now, it is up to Glitch, a mysterious droid found deactivated in a dumpster, to stop him.
