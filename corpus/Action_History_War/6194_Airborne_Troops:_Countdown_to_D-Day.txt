Relive the 48 hours preceding the landing of the allies in Normandy (from 4 till 6 June at dawn). Airborne Troops is a 3D real-time game of shooter/infiltration - seen to the 3rd person. The adventure takes place in an occupied France during the second world war. Occupied France is not a perfect place to land for an unprepared allied soldier. It's nevertheless that John, a young American officer, member of the paratroopers, has made. Filled with awesome gunfights and challenging strategic dilemmas, airborne troops will keep you enthralled until completion. So strap on your parachute sergeant, it's time to earn those stripes.
