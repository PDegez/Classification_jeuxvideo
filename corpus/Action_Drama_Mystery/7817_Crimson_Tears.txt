The year is 2049. Tokyo suddenly became a world of labyrinth. The super weapons manufacturer A.R.M.A. has gone out of control when the accident caused the distortions in the planet's spatial dimensions to destabilize. Three Mutanoids are sent by the A.R.M.A. to face various dangerous situations of the dimensional matters. Amber, a female Mutanoid specializes in wielding twin blades with every kinds of bladed weapons. Tokio, a male Mutanoid specializes in fire arms. And Kadie, another female Mutanoid specializes in various close range hand-to-hand combat including claws and Buster Swords. These Mutanoids must use their wits and strengths to finding out what was the cause of the dimensional incident and uncover various mysteries as well. Will they succeed?
