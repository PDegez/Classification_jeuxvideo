While excavating under the Kremlin, archaeologists discovered a mysterious wooden mansion. Something disturbed the evil spirit of the ancient masters - The forces of evil wake up under the ground, mutant bats multiplied - One by one the brave men went to meet the danger and did not return - Now there is only one hope - A brave digger descends into the excavation. He will be bolder and more circumspect than his unfortunate predecessors. His task - to save the city from disaster.
