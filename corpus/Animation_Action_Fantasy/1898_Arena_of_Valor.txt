Arena of Valor, formerly Realm of Valor then Strike of Kings, is an international adaptation of Wangzhe Rongyao "variably known in English unofficial translations as King of Glory, Kings of Glory, Honor of King, or Honour of King", a multiplayer online battle arena developed and published by Tencent Games for the iOS, Android and Nintendo Switch, for markets outside mainland China. As of September 2018, the game has grossed over $890 million outside China.
