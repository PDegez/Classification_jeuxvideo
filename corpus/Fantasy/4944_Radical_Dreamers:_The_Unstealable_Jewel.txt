Welcome to Viper Manor, a place that hides many secrets to be unfolded, some of them maybe only unlockable in the future... Serge, Kid and Magil crawl into the depths of the apparent castle to retrieve the lost treasure - the so-called Frozen Flame - in this text-scrolling RPG genre installment. They are also led to unravel the sinister ambitions from the ones behind the curtain that keep the treasure at bay, and also the many choices and respective results which awaits them inside!
