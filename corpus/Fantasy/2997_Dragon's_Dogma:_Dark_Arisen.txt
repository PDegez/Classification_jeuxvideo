The end of days is near, and dragons have returned to Gransys. Become the chosen Arisen and decide the fate of the world in Dragon's Dogma: Dark Arisen, the critically acclaimed Action-RPG now updated for current generation consoles. Based in an epic fantasy setting, Dragon's Dogma: Dark Arisen combines exhilarating and deep combat with the freedom to explore and interact in a rich, living and breathing world. Choose between nine different classes and embark on your own adventure along with three AI companions, known as Pawns. These allies fight independently, develop alongside your character, and can be shared or borrowed online in exchange for treasure, tips and more.
