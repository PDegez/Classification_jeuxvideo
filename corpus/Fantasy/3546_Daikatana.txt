Mikiko Ebihara, beautiful daughter of the unfortunate Dr. Toshiro Ebihara, has a favor to ask you. Will you join her in the quest to regain the mystical Daikatana, avenge her father's death, and set history straight? Journey from a future Kyoto, Japan, to ancient Greece, then on to Norway in the Middle Ages before meeting the ultimate challenges in San Francisco, 2030 A.D.
