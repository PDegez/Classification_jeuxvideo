A young woman, Elena, gets cursed during the Harvest Festival, and is slowly turning into a horrible beast. A young man, Aeron, saves her from the military and travels, along with a old woman, to the great abyss called the Scar. In the middle of the Scar, a great fortress is suspended, with 13 towers. Deep inside these towers lies the cure to Elena's curse. Aeron will have to travel to these towers to collect the cure and save Elena.
