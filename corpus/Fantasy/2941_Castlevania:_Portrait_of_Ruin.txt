In 1944, the world is engulfed in the flames of World War II, its horror and strife bringing darkness into the hearts of the people, and the bitter cries of its dead have again given rise to Dracula's Castle. In the midst of this Jonathan Morris (son of Jon Morris, who slew Dracula in "Bloodlines") and Charlotte Aulin (a witch and Jonathan's childhood friend) have arrived to investigate and find a way to stop the Dark Lord from rising once again...
