This game is divided into 6 chapters. The first one, Ragnar is tasked to find the missing children. The second, a tomboy Princess named Alena escapes from her castle with 2 allies for adventure. The third, a merchant named Taloon sets off to establish his own store business. The fourth 2 Sisters Maya and Meena seeks revenge on Keeleon who killed their father. The fifth features a hero who's hometown gets destroyed and he gathers 7 of the allies to fight for him to defeat Estark in this turn based RPG game. The sixth, is a secret chapter for after beating the game.
