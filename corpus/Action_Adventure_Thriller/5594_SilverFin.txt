The Young Bond "SilverFin" game follows the story of the best-selling novel where an adolescent James has just started boarding school at Eton in the 1930's. From there, the action moves to the Highlands of Scotland, where Alfie Kelly, a local boy, has gone missing. Young Bond teams up with the boy's cousin, Red, to investigate the mystery and soon discovers that Alfie's disappearance is linked to a madman and his sinister plot for global power.
