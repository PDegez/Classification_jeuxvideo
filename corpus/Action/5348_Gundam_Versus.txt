After six years of version updates to Extreme Vs., Gundam Versus marks a fully new entry, designed for home consoles, in the long running Vs. series. The base game starts with 94 playable units spanning 40 years of Gundam anime, manga, and games, including Vs. series first appearances for Reconguista in G, Iron-Blooded Orphans, MSG Thunderbolt, and Gundam Build Fighters. Additional playable units are rolled out on a regular schedule as paid DLC.
