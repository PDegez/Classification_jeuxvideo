GUNDAM BREAKER 2 includes 100 Gundam model units which far exceed the previous title. Collect parts by destroying the enemy Gundam model and create your own Gundam model. You can also further enhance your favorite unit with the new development system which just got introduced in this title. Enjoy the dynamic war action with the destruction from the mega beam and the footing during the battle with battleships and giant MA, as well as the Dendrobium fight.
