Hotel Giant is a simulation game much like the well-known The Sims. You as the hotel manager have to show your managing skills and prove yourself in the hotel and tourism industry. As long as you make sure your guests are happy, success is guaranteed. Own and manage your own hotel, from catering, creating an entertainment program for your guests like arcade games or gamling, make sure everyting is okay in the hotel rooms of the guests and much more. If you loved The Sims, you'll like Hotel Giant as well.
