Following the defeat of Bilstein and the Fourth Empire in Final Crusade, both make an unexpected return but the appearance of Bilstein in a cybernetic body is followed by the ghost of Bilstein in his old cybernetic body. Meanwhile, the Fourth Empire has rebuilt its power and is determined to carry out Bilstein's evil will. Hayato, June, Saturn, and Gamof must head back into the relentless fight in order to defeat Bilstein and the Fourth Empire once and for all.
