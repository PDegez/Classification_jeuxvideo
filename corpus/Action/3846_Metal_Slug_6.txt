Marco Rossi and Tarma Roving, the Peregrine Falcons, have once again joined Eri Kasamoto and Fiolina Germi of the S.P.A.R.R.O.W.S. to take on General Morden, who has formed another alliance with the Martians. However, it is soon revealed that the Martians are now being targeted by a new and dangerous race of aliens known as the Invaders. Now, the four must join forces with Morden and the Martians to wipe out the Invaders and save the world.
