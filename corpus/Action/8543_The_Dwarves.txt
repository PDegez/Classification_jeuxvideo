The Dwarves is a fantasy role-playing game with a strong story and tactically challenging real-time battles. Fifteen playable heroes, each with individual skills, are to be deployed cleverly. Take on superior numbers of orcs, ogres, älfs, bögnilim, zombies, dark mages and many more foes, to give them the low-down. Based on the bestselling novel "The Dwarves" by Markus Heitz you'll experience a fascinating tale revolving around the coolest fantasy race ever: The Dwarves.
