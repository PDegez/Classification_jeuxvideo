Suburban Commando is based on the 1991 movie of the same name, starring professional wrestling superstar Hulk Hogan. He plays Shep Ramsey, an intergalactic do-gooder, chased by two bounty hunters of the evil General Suitor. Sheps ship crash-lands in the suburbs of Los Angeles. He finds shelter with the Wilcox family and subsequently tries to blend in, but the bounty hunters are tracking him down, so Shep must protect the Wilcox and himself.
