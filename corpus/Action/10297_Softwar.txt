John Doe, a serious and young self-employed programmer who lives alone, accidentally downloads a program that changes his life forever: The ILOVEYOU Virus. Having his computer infected by a virus is the last thing a programmer wants. Unless, the virus actually turns out to be useful - Find out more about ILOVEYOU Virus or "ILY" as John navigates through the unknown realms of the cyber-world. Follow their adventures as they continue to come across new artificial intelligence programs and engage in virtual battles known as Softwars.
