SOCOM U.S. Navy Seals: Fireteam Bravo 3 is a tactical 3rd person shooter made for the "PSP" handheld console by Slant Six Games. You play the main protagonist Wraith (voiced by Khary Payton) as he and his team of Navy Seals are sent to Koratvia to track down Vasyli Gozorov: an Ex-KGB operative with known terrorist links.
