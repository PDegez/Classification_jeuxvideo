You've heard about the magical disturbance. Too many people died to it. People here and there went out to find a way to end it. All of them failed. Heroes - Pilgrims - Armies - You can imagine how disastrous it will be if this cataclysm keeps growing. You know you have to do it yourself: You learn about the location of the disturbance's source. You walk your way there, on your own. And here you are now.
