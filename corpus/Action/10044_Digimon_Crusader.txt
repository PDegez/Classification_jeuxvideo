The supercomputer housing the Digital World, Yggdrasill, suddenly vanished plunging the Digital World into chaos. Digimon are trying to gain power to become the "Next Ruler of the Digital World" so Angewomon has decided to call coaches from the Real World (equipped with cards) to re-establish law and order. Off In the forest, originally far away from the wars, Aegiomon was living in peace with his friends until Titamon and his army of Orgemon invaded. Now he's drawn into war and has a crucial role to play in the upcoming struggle.
