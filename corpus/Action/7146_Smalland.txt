"There was a time when we lived freely under the Sun and Moon, before the time of the giants. This history has passed down from generation to generation so we can see them again, even if only in dreams. But now we are ready to return to the surface and you, vanguard, will lead the way..." Explore the vast "Land of the Small". Traverse lake sized puddles, scale skyscraper sized trees, battle or tame creatures, discover the dense dark forests and the strange metallic monoliths left over from before.
