Cookie Cutter tells the story of a Denzel, an android built as a sex doll by one of the most brilliant scientists in existence, Dr. Shinji. A story of love and revenge which is revealed layer by layer using the multitude of diverse game areas. Players will gradually discover Cherry and Regina's story as well as learn of Salem Garbanzos, the sole heir of the large Infonet corporation. The upcoming demo will allow you to explore Sektor 299, the stage of an incredible and sinister story where many details can be discovered, revealing the secrets that lie behind hostile and silent corridors.
