Natas, the alien king, has died and now the Ora stone is in the hands of the people of Oddesia, safely. Peace has been restored and evil has been prevented for many years until Fades a follower of Natas resurrects him and seals the Ora stone, causing Oddesia to be in danger, also the princess Miriam of Solis has been captured by Mynosan evil monster, it is up to you to help eight characters get the Ora stone back and save princess Miriam.
