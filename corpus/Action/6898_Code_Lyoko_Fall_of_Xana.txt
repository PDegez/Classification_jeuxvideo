Fall of Xana picks up our super hero school chums where they left off. Our heroes have to save their friend William, all while protecting the worlds of Earth and Lyoko and finishing their homework. Combining high-tech gadgetry and adrenaline-packed missions, this innovative blend of adventure, combat is packed with new monsters, intense mini games and puzzles. The fate of Lyoko is up to you as you try to eradicate the XANA virus once and for all.
