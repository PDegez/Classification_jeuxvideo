Flushy Fish VR: The Story of a Fearless Prince Who Was Turned Into a Fish Faraway in the Golden Star Galaxy once lived a happy Kingdom of Caroba ruled by King Bubbles and Queen Barbara who had a newborn child Prince Flushy. From the shadows of the Golden Star once came a witch-king Norman who wanted to enslave Caroba and take over the magical golden stars. To defeat Caroba Norman turned prince Flushy into a fish, and sent him to the deepest place in the world, where he could never find the way back home. His monstrous forces reclaimed Caroba, invaded their land and they are now ready to take on the rest of the world with mayhem. There was one last hope - Trinity, the explosive fairy who could find Flushy and guide him back home to save the kingdom.
