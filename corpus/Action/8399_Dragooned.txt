Dragooned is a single player pick-up-and-play action game with 40 playable characters that have very different play-styles and a variety of modes, including advancing through procedurally generated levels with a party of characters, facing down endless onslaughts of enemies and undertaking a variety of challenges. The differing character abilities allow for a vast range of play-styles, from basic offensive, defensive, speedy and projectile focuses to summoning, aerial combat, area control, status effects, knock-back, counterattacks, stealth, teleportation and even more advanced play-styles beyond.
