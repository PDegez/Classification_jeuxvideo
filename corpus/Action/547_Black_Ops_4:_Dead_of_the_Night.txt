England, 1912. Friends and acquaintances of relic-hunter Alistair Rhodes flock to his country estate for a grand gala. Among them: a phony psychic, a stage-show gunslinger, a retired brigadier general, and Alistair's faithful butler. But when the festivities turn into an undead bloodbath, Madame Mirela, Gideon Jones, Brigadier Jonathan Warwick and Godfrey must unite against an army of gothic horrors.
