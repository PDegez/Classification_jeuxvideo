The Sega Game Gear version of "The Adventures of Batman and Robin" is a platforming brawler game. When the Joker kidnaps Robin, Batman must fight his way through four levels of goons and villains in order to rescue him. Along the way he must defeat the Mad Hatter, Mr. Freeze, Scarecrow, Harley Quinn, and finally the Joker using punches, kicks, and an unlimited supply of Batarangs.
