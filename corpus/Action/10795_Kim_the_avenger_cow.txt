Kim the Avenger Cow is a fresh action side-scroll-er. You are Kim, a very unusual and angry cow. Defeat your enemies with unlimited squirts of acid milk. Discover what's behind the evil dairy industry in this fast paced adventure, packed with a lot of minigames and wacky animations. As farmers worldwide engaged in a never ending race to compete for the highest productivity, they started to test all sort of ideas. One farm, had an amazing idea: Mutate some milk-producing genes, to make a cow capable of producing infinite amounts of milk. Kim was the healthiest, strongest cow in the farm. She had a very bad temper, but that was only another display of her strength. And she was the perfect candidate to test the new genetic serum.
