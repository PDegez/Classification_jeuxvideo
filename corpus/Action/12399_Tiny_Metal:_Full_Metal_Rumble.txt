Commander Wolfram has chased the shadow of her brother to "Land's End," a treacherous mountain range packed heavy with snow. As her investigation into her sibling's disappearance leads her closer to the quarry, Wolfram's enemies rain down a siege of bullets and heavy artillery upon her. Leaving the fierce, independent commander at a standstill. As Wolfram struggles to find her brother, Artemisian Captain Nathan Gries pursues Lost Tech artifacts, while the Zipanese Lord General Tsukumo begins his hunt for a mysterious new enemy hiding a dangerous agenda. A new stage is set, and the fate of these three entwine on the battlefield.
