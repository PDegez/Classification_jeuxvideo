The Men in Black need you to join their elite team and help protect the Earth from the scum of the universe. In Men in Black II: Crossfire, you'll be armed with a variety of powerful gadgets and weapons in order to eliminate a group of aliens wreaking havoc on human civilization. After being trained in the MIB headquarters, you will be equipped with the latest technology to effectively handle any mission that comes your way. The MIB are looking for the best of the best. Are you up to the challenge?
