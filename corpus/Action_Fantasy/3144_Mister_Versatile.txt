The Lone Wolf Mister Versatile thinks super teams are a pain in the rear end, but four super-hunks have decided they want to watch his back anyway. Mister Versatile is screwed, and not in the good way at all. The Bad Guys have penetrated his tender headquarters. Will Mr. Versatile finally let down his guard and get the help he needs, and if so, then which superhero will teach him how to play nice with others?
