Set in the medieval Balkans, the player's ultimate goal is to defeat a ruthless bolyar seeking the throne. Bogarash is a hack and slash role-playing game using pre-rendered sprites in isometric perspective. It features: experience, inventory, dialogue, loot and skills systems. Fog of war and exploration. Room transitions and Zoom in/out. Save/Load and basic options. Localization.Ini file, support cyrillic script.
