The Machine, And Her released on September 8th, 2019 for PC and Xbox. Set in post-apocalyptic America, the player character receives a strange broadcast announcing the "Grand Opening" of a decrepit subway station. Upon arrival they meet Kit, a teenage girl who has befriended A.G.N.I.S., the "Automated Transit Steward" who still announces the arrival and departure of trains, unaware that the world ended over 150 years ago. As the player and Kit travel across the wasteland together on a mission to repair A.G.N.I.S. the player learns about Kit's disturbed past that links her fate with that of the AI, and must then decide the outcome of both their stories.
