Spectral Force Genesis is a combination of war simulator, real time strategy and role playing game. The game gives you the command over an army of a broken down kingdom. It's up to you through battles and communication with the people from your units to let your kingdom be as strong as it used to be. The game also includes more managerial options, such as the taxes and diplomacy of your country. The game can be fully played with the stylus of the Nintendo DS.
