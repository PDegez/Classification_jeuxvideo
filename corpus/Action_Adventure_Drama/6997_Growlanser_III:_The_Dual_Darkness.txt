An eerie darkness engulfs the world, purging it of sunlight. Plague and famine begin to sweep through towns and villages. Four neighboring countries turn enemy as they seek to hoard what little provisions the dying earth can spare. In the midst of it all, a man named Slayn Wilder awakens with amnesia. As political scandal and treachery ensnare the leaders of the continent, Slayn's increasing involvement in the war effort slowly begins to gnaw away at the curtain obstructing his past, and it soon becomes clear that he will make all the difference in the fight for survival...
