Taken place in the settings of World War II of the battle against the Nazis led by Adolf Hitler, Edward Kyle and his friend Jude Lancelot are enlisted in the British Special Forces led by Lieutenant Colonel James Gallant after Edward was saved from a near fatal gunshot attack via blood transfusion. Later, Edward finds out that the blood transfusion gave him Military Spirits which enables him to use the power of a beast. At first, Edward decided to leave the British Special Forces, but changed his mind and must help the British Special Forces end the tyranny of the Nazis who are joined by the vampire soldiers led by the Sturmbannfuehrer, Alexander Vlado.
