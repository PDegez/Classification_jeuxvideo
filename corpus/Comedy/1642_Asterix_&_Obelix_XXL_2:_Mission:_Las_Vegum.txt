Asterix and Obelix XXL 2: Mission: Las Vegum is an action- game for Windows and PlayStation 2, the sequel to Asterix and Obelix XXL. It was released in June 2006 in Europe. The game stars the French comic book characters Asterix and Obelix, and features spoofs to many classic games, in the same fashion that the comics spoof historical characters and make many cultural references.
