alien front online be a tank driving shoot em up that focus on online play the premise be a classic alien invasion of earth non friendly alien descend to the earth and put up stronghold around globe you can choose to fight the result battle from the side of the army or the side of the alien every faction have three vehicle small medium and large with different armor and speed in online mode this game support 4 player vs 4 player battle a special catch be the usage of the dreamcast microphone which allow you to speak either with all player or only your own team in single player mode there be 30 mission for each side you move from mission to mission in a branch storyline with multiple ending additionally there be an arcade mode in which the goal be to kill all alien in a limited time
