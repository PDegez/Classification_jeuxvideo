Superman: The Game is a game of nail-biting strategy and stunning action. The game' setting in Metropolis, where Darkseid's reign of terror has begun. Darkseid seeks to capture the citizens while Superman tries to save them. Once all the people of Metropolis have been captured or saved, the player with the most people is the winner. However, if during play either Superman or Darkseid's energy levels fall to zero, the surviving character wins the game.
