WWE All Stars is a video game in which you can live out your wrestling fantasy's. Enter the Path Of Champions to defeat WWE legends such as the WWE champion Randy Orton, the heavyweight champion The Undertaker and the comedic tag team champion duo Degeneration X. Simulate matches that never have happened until now in Fantasy Warfare such as: Eddie Guerrero VS Rey Mysterio, Kofi Kingston VS Ricky "The Dragon" Steamboat, Andre The Giant VS The Big Show. And much much more.
