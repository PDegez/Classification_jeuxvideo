Description: A gorgeous beautiful toy that will please all children and nostalgic adults. In the game, you play for Ariel - the naughty daughter of the King of the Sea of Triton. Everywhere you will be waiting for friends and enemies. You will be fascinated by a fabulous underwater world, as well as by land. At each level, you need to collect treasures. At the Little Mermaid (Ariel) a bunch of helpers, and she herself knows how to open chests and fight enemies with a punch of the tail, and in some places even pierce the walls with her head.
