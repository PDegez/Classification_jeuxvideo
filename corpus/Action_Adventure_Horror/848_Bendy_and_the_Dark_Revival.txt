Audrey is an animator working at Joey Drew Studios. After being taken by a mysterious man named Wilson, who claims to have vanquished the Ink Demon 211 days before, she finds herself in an inky form in the world of the ink machine. Audrey must now navigate the studio, ally with iconic cartoon characters such as a hardened Alice, a tough Boris, and even a 'perfect' Bendy. And she must learn to avoid the terrifying new Ink Demon and escape from the realm she is trapped within.
