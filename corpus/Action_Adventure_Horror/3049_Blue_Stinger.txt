Elliot Ballade is an ESER member taking a Christmas Vacation at Dinosaur Island in the year 2018. However, some monsters attacked at his boat and drifted ashore. The whole island is full of monsters. Aided by the boatowner, Dogs Bowers, and chief of security, Janine King, Elliot and Dogs try to find out why the monsters are on Dinosaur Island and try to eliminate the threat.
