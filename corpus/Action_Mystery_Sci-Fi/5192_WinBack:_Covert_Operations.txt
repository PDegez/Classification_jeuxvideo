After the first blast from the satellite wiped out the Space Center, The Secretary of Defense of the Pentagon calls for a special military team S.C.A.T. to stop the terrorist known as the "Crying Lions." During the briefing on the S.C.A.T. chopper, there was an engine failure and everyone team parachuted out of the chopper but they all got separated. As Jean-Luc Cougar, you must stop the terrorist group and take back the control of the satellite from the terrorists. Jean-Luc also must find and rescue his teammates at the same time as well. Will Jean-Luc succeed in completing the mission?
