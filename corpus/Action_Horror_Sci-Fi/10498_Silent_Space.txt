Aboard the derelict BETA-43 space station, your only hope for survival is to just keep moving forward. Death is all but certain and you have nothing but your wits and your meager tools to survive. What unleashed the horrors upon this station? What happened to the crew? Discover the truth as you try to avoid detection and make your way to the escape pods. Silent Space is a horror metroidvania made for the Themed Horror Game Jam #6 in 1 month by a group of game developers from all around the globe.
