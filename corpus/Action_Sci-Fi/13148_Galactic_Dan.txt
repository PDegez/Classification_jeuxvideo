In the far future, the peaceful moon of Lygon has been invaded by a robotic alien race known as the Vorians. The inhabitants of the moon, the Poloids, have been kidnapped and are being held as hostages. Galactic Dan Quicksmart, an agent of the United Planets Bureau of Investigations is being sent to the moon to rescue the hostages. On his mission, he has to search through ruined cities, cave dwellings, and Vorian installations while watching out for the robot guardians that are programmed to kill anyone that opposes them.
