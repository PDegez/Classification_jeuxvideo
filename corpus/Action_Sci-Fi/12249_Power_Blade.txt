In the year 2191, planet Earth has recovered after a long war that caused millions of casualties. Ruled by Master Computer, people lead a pacific existence until the a day an alien race attacks Master Computer. Malfunctioning, Master Computer launches an attack on the six sectors of the megalopolis. The Chief of Security summons Nova, a master of the ancient Power Blade (a special boomerang with high destructive capacities), to defeat the aliens and recover Master Computer. *You* travel across the six levels, increases your boomerang's power, find your contacts, locate the armor suit, get the I.D. Card, destroy the bosses of each level and confront the alien leader. You are the only hope to save planet Earth.
