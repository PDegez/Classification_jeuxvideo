The Scranderlants are an alien life form known for being one of the deadliest creatures in the galaxy. The Scranderlants have begun to make hundreds of thousands of Droids to overpower their enemy in battle. However, one Droid, called the Green Man, gains consciousness right before he is about to be teleported into battle. Wanting to have a life of his own, the Green Man will do anything he can to escape from the Scranderlants.
