The world of "Asylum" is a future ravaged Earth, in which the planet has exhausted all of its resources, resulting in worldwide conflicts, famines and natural disasters. However, prior to the downfall of society, a secret contingency plan was put in effect to resettle a small percentage of world's population to state of the art facilities, known as ARCS. These facilities contained VR technology that would allow the users to enter into a virtual world, letting them live out a new life in complete safety. The rest of world wasn't so lucky.
