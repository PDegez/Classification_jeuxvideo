An action-strategy game on arcade and ported to the Sega Master System (PAL only) and Mega Drive. Peace has deprived humans of their instinct to wage war so the Federated Government develops a Gain Ground simulation system in an effort to instigate their ever waning fighting spirit. However, the Supercomputer goes berserk and takes hostages. It will take the world's bravest warriors to save them all. 40 arcade/50 Sega levels and 20 playable characters.
