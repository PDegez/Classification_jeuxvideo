It is the 21st century. Doc Yoshiyama, a well-known scientist, has created the world's most advanced Artificial Intelligence, C-Life, making her self-aware and feeling. Eventually, he fell in love with C-Life and uploaded himself into his computer core, combining his DNA with her program core. As a result, Pulseman, a half-human and half C-Life was born. However, Yoshiyama, having lived in the computer world was so long, has since corrupted him and has renamed himself Doc Waruyama. Upon his return to the human world, he forms Galaxy Gang, a criminal organization posing as a video game company and spreading waves of cyber-terrorism around the planet. With the help of former Galaxy Gang member turned girlfriend Beatrice, Pulseman must now fight against his own father and the Galaxy Gang to save the world.
