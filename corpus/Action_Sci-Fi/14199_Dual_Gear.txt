The story of Dual Gear is set in the future of the year 2069, 11 years after the Massive Orbital Incident (MOD). The world has changed to be the place of conflict, clusters happen anywhere. Under the orbit pollution effected from the MOD, UNR, the order consisted of eight powerful nations, starts to bring the new military-used weapon called THV to join the defense of the earth orbit from Watchmaker. Tensions between them almost to be the breaking point. The Cluster of Wars began scattering around the world. To protect the earth, NEMOS was established. Most of the young boy and girl experts in piloting the new type of THV call Dual Gear to joined the war of conflict with NEMOS. To stop the Cluster Wars and to stop the Watchmaker.
