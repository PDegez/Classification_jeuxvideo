It's the year 2492, You are a S.T.U.N. Runner and must pilot your Sub-Terranean Underground Network (S.T.U.N.) craft at speeds over 900 MPH through a series of courses to retain your status as S.T.U.N. Runner. completing each course before time runs out. There are also obstacles you must shot or avoid, like cycles, indestructible armored drones, and flying crafts that will try and bomb you.
