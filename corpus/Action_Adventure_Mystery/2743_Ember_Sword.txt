Once an advanced, colonized moon of the interplanetary government of Pangaea, all was changed for Thanabus when a godlike entity called Starzhen triggered an apocalyptic event dubbed the "Catastrophe". In the wake of the devastation, the survivors rallied around the young pupil of the late Governor and created a provisional government called the Republic. Hundreds of years later, its rule perseveres, yet new forces have risen and challenge its rule over the northern hemisphere. Around every corner, unseen machinations are afoot and there are more questions than ever before. Will you unravel the great mystery?
