To find a mysterious artifact - the legacy of the ancient gods - and with his help to decide the fate of the universe, the main heroine of the game Ayumi has to go through many serious tests. The loyal companions of the heroine are two gangblades, who are merciless to enemies in close combat and are effective as firearms. The deadly power of the charming adventurer increases thanks to the spells of several schools of magic. In the battles with numerous enemies and huge bosses Ayumi will gain experience, so necessary for the discovery of new abilities and spells.
