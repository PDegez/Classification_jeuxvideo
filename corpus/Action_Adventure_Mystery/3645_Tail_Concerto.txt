Waffle Ryebread, a police dog, whose vacation is interrupted when he gets a call from headquarters saying that the Black Cats Gang are attacking Resaca. Waffle with his trusty Police Robo must pursue the Black Cats Gang and stop them from harming the dog-people. But things don't look easy for Waffle as he didn't noticed that his old neighbor Alicia Pris, the leader of the Black Cats Gang is responsible for all of the mess. Can Waffle be able to stop the Black Cats Gang and be able to make up with Alicia and the cat-people?
