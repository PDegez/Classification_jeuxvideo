A group of 25 unlikely heroes band together and fight to protect the last star in the universe from the Varelsi, an evil organization with the purpose to destroy every last universe and leave the galaxy in total darkness. From the studio that brought you the borderlands series, a new threat arises, and the Battleborn must rise to the challenge or face defeat. With an in depth progression system and incredible story and multiplayer modes, Battleborn releases May 3rd.
