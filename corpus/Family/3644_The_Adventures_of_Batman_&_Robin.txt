The Super Nintendo version of "The Adventures of Batman and Robin" is primarily a side-scrolling platform brawler, with a top-down driving stage. Batman travels around Gotham in order to take down a rogue's gallery of thugs and classic villains, including: the Joker, Poison Ivy, the Penguin, Catwoman, Two-Face, Scarecrow, the Riddler, Clayface, and Man-Bat. Each of the eight levels have a different style and gameplay mechanic based on the end-boss, providing variety to the game.
