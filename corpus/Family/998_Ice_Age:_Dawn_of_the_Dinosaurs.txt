We present to your attention a computer game, which was created based on one of the most famous animated films. You are waiting for a completely new  of the famous pair of mammoths Manny and Ellie, but so the developers did not forget about the epic sloth Side, the dangerous die-shed tiger Diego and many other memorable characters from the third part of the Ice Age. Trying to pass through a dangerous tundra, our heroes managed to discover a wonderful tropical world, which was hidden under a huge thickness of ice. And this world was inhabited by real dinosaurs.
