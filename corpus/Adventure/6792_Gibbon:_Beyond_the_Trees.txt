Master the ape's art of brachiation (swinging from branch to branch) at high speed, building momentum, pulling off spectacular somersaults, and launching yourself higher and further into the air with the help of your fellow apes, feeling the thrill and exhilaration of life in an untamed jungle. Gibbon: Beyond the Trees deals with challenging real world environmental issues, including deforestation, poaching and climate change that threatens the life of gibbons. Game include: - Master free-flowing dynamic movement based on brachiation, the way real apes swing through the trees - Build momentum through dazzlingly acrobatic jumps, including launching from another gibbon's hands in mid air - Experience the affecting tale of a family of gibbons trying to survive amid an encroaching human threat. - Play through an hour long adventure in story mode, or race to freedom through a procedurally generated jungle in liberation mode. - Stunning 2D hand painted visuals that breathe life into a rapidly vanishing world.
