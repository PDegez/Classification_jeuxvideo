Batman is a 3D isometric action- game by Ocean Software, and the first Batman game developed. Batman's sidekick Robin has been kidnapped by the Joker and the Riddler while he was repairing the Batmobile. But first, Batman must find seven missing parts of the bat-hovercraft that Robin left scattered throughout the Batcave and Bruce's mansion. Batman also has to start off without his equipment that he must find.
