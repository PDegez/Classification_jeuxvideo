This is an arcade shoot 'em up video game. Turbo Girl, well known throughout the Galaxy, lieutenant of the immediate intervention corps of the Korg army, has been chosen to fulfill a suicide mission: Three "Elder" (strange cyber beings) intercept colony supplies supported by an army of ships -robot; She must destroy them. She must cross the different phases avoiding being destroyed by the robot-ships and ends with the "Elder" that watches each one of them.
