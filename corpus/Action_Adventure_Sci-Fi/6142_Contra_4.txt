The year is 2638, two years after the Alien Wars had come to a close. The Red Falcon threat had been neutralized, and Earth is once again in peace. However, a similar alien called Black Viper begun launching attacks on the planet, causing mass destruction. After strange readings were detected at the Galuga archipelago, the Earth Federation sends in four commandos, Bill Rizer, Lance Bean, Mad Dog, and Scorpion, to infiltrate the island and defeat Black Viper and its army of aliens and soldiers.
