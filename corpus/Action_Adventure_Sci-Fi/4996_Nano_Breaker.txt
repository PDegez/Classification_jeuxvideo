When nanotechnology goes out of control, innocent people are horrendously mutated into Orgamechs, a nanotechnology mutants that would viciously kill anyone with no control of its own self. With this happening, General Raymond sends in a humanoid cyborg, Jake Warren to take care of the onslaught committed by various Orgamechs. Armed with a plasma blade that can form various weapons shapes and rip apart any Orgamechs in seconds. Jake was also given an assignment by General Raymond to find a woman named Michelle Baker. At the same time, Jake would eventually run into his nemesis Keith Spencer who Jake needs to fend off against. Will Jake be able to end the nanotechnology gone AWOL?
