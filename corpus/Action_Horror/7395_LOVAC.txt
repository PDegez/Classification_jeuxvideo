The year is 2063. Corporations have grown larger than ever to the point where even the Police are just another company trying to make more money than the others. In LOVAC you take the role of a world weary cop named Sam Cooper. With His troubled past the last thing Sam wants is more blood on his hands. However, When responding to reports of a Siege on The Grey Line corporations skyscraper it becomes apparent that He may not have a choice in the matter. Little does Cooper know that tonight will force Him to face more than His personal demons - But also Unforeseen Consequences.
