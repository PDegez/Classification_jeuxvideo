Jack Russell, a 16 year-old boy, wants to become a knight just like his late father, Cairn. On the day that Radiata Castle are trying to recruit the people who wants to become the Radiata knights. Jack was so excited that he departs from his sister's home in Solieu Village and enters Radiata Castle to prove his might to participate of joining the knights. When the announcer calls Jack to the coliseum, he also announced that a girl named Ridley Silverlake will be dueling against Jack who thought the name Ridley was a man's name. Just as he enters the coliseum to face his opponent, Jack was confused to find out that Ridley was his first opponent. Just as Jack and Ridley duel each other, Jack was instantly defeated by Ridley with her Wild Pitch attack. Afterwards, Junzaburo announces who will be the Radiata Knights. The announcement was that Ganz Rothschild, Ridley Silverlake and for some unknown fortunate reason, Jack was selected to become one of them even though he was defeated by Ridley during the duel. During the battle against the Red Orc, Ridley was badly wounded from the Red Orc's attack.
