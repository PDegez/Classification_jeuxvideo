Portable version of Rayman 3, released for the Game Boy Advance. Globox swallows a Dark Lum while Rayman was trying to save the Teensies. As Rayman is about to go and find a way to cure him, Globox runs away, and leaves him worried that he will end up doing something stupid. Meanwhile, Admiral Razorbeard hears about this incident and becomes hellbent on catching Globox to extract the Dark Lum, so he can use it to make himself more powerful.
