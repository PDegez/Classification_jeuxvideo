Tales of Vesperia focuses on the world, called Terca Lumireis. In these world, people use an energy source called blastia for all their needs, including creating protective barriers around cities. The story focuses on Yuri Lowell, a former paladin, who forms a guild called Brave Vesperia to aid Estelle, a noble woman he encounters on a mission. As they explore the world, Brave Vesperia is challenged by factions that have different plans related to the abuse of blastia resources. Yuri is forced to confront his former friends, as well as his former comrade Flynn.
