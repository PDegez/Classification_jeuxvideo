A great tense wait between Karus and El-Morad. Both race left behind the peaceful tradeland of Moradon. El-Moradian leader King Manes commands his army to erase Orcs of Karus. But even the Karus are primitive, they are also physically too strong for Men. The great war begins in Lunar Valley and ends in El-Morad Castle or Luferson Castle. Which race will won Men or Orcs? Even The Great Oracle Beckyr can't see who wins The Great Lunar War and who captures The Great Trio: Iron Bow, Raptor and Shard.
