Its the Primitive Human BeingS Versus the Advance Alien Race in this Epic Video Game of the Century. You are on a Quest to rescue the Princess and Kick some Alien Tail in this Epic Galatic Adventure of a Lifetime "KRULL". On the planet of Krull, an evil creature called the Beast decimates the world's army and kidnaps the lovely Princess Lyssa , who is destined to become queen. Her brave beau, Prince Colwyn , leads a motley band of warriors, including Ergo and Kegan , to rescue his beloved. However, before he can face the Beast, Colwyn must locate a mystical weapon known as the Glaive, which he can use to slay the hideous villain.
