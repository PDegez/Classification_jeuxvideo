Free Infantry is set in the universe of Infantry chronicles, a crumbling empire known as the Collective against a tide of independence movements, the Collective unified Earth after the holy wars of 2200 and expanded humanity throughout the solar system in a series of colonies as far as Pluto. Infantry offers an unparalleled all out frenetic gaming experience where hundreds of players battle with skill, strategy and team work. Infantry is a massively multiplayer online (MMO) 2D action game referred to as the world's first and only isometric shooter.
