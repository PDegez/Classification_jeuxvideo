Biwar Legend of Dragon Slayer is an Action-Adventure Puzzle game that invites you to explore the puzzles using magic mechanic such a: Telekinesis Power, Agni and Toya elements and Non Euclidean puzzles. The third-person game will give you experience of fight the Mythology Creatures in a fantasy realm, The game is set in South East Asia culture of Balinese ancient environment and Papuans folklore are the unique of Nusantara Culture.
